What is the Algorithmic Impact Assessment?

The AIA is a questionnaire designed to help you assess and mitigate the risks associated with deploying an automated decision system. The AIA also helps identify the impact level of your automated decision system under the proposed Directive on Automated Decision-Making. The questions are focused on your business processes, your data, and your system design decisions.

How does it work?

The questionnaire asks you around 57 questions related to your business process, data and system designed decisions. The results will demonstrate your impact level as well as a link where you can learn about what your requirements are under the Directive. The questionnaire takes 35 minutes to complete.

Where can I find out why my system was rated a certain level?

Click "Detailed Results" below and for a detailed scoresheet showing exactly where you gathered risk. You can download your results.

Can I save my responses and complete the questionnaire later?

Yes. You can save your spot at any time by clicking "Save progress to a file". It will download a tiny JSON file, which you can use to reload your results when you're ready to proceed. We don't save any of your information.

Do you store my answers?

No. We don't store your responses. Feel free to use this tool as many times as you need along the development of your system.
